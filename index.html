    What is MJPJAY?

    15 March, 2023

    Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY):Mahatma Jyotirao Pule Jan Arogya Yojana is a flagship health insurance scheme of Government of Maharashtra. The scheme provides end to end cashless services for identified diseases through a network of service providers from Government and Private sector. The scheme earlier was known as Rajiv Gandhi Jeevandayee Arogya Yojana which was started from 2nd July,2012 in eight districts and then was expanded to 28 districts of Maharashtra from 21st November, 2013.

    Visit Official

    Who is eligible for MJPJAY?


    The Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana eligibility requirements demand individuals meet the following parameters to qualify as beneficiaries:
    • Individual applicants must have an annual income of less than Rs.1 lakh.
    • Family members of the primary applicant can enjoy Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana coverage benefits as well.
    • Registering families must belong to the identified distressed districts of Maharashtra.
    • Farmers belonging to agriculturally distressed districts of Maharashtra can also sign up for this insurance policy.
    Once you meet the above eligibility requirements, you will need to submit specific documents to support your claims.


    Features of MJPJAY


    Here is a list of features of the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana:

    • This health insurance scheme offers financial coverage up to Rs.1.5 lakhs per year against medical treatments.
    • Policyholders do not need to deposit any sum while undergoing treatment, so it is a cashless process.
    • The State Government of Maharashtra bears the premium charges required for Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana.
    • This scheme allows an individual beneficiary to benefit from it. Alternatively, an entire family can also enjoy insurance benefits with a floater policy.
    • The Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana scheme does not specify any waiting period for pre-existing ailments.
    • Policyholders can also attend 1 medical camp per year free of cost.

    If you want to check the accessibility of such healthcare benefits near you, look up the list of network hospitals. It is available on the official website of the State Health Assurance Society. You can find a number of private as well as government-enrolled hospitals in this list. All of these can offer you the benefits of Mahatma Phule Arogya Yojana.
